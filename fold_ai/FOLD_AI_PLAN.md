## THE MISSION (Phase I statement)

Rebuild the AI stack on fold mathematics: replace statistically-trained
structure with DERIVED structure wherever law exists, train only the truly
contingent residual, and cash the difference as computation and scale.

The thesis (the fold's, stated flat): a trained network's weights encode a
function whose lawful part is a compact object in the right coordinates.
Training is a statistical purchase of structure that derivation gets free.

The existence proof already on the board: the chess campaign. A
zero-parameter counted engine — no training, no weights, no book — beat
Stockfish's 1900 setting and drew half its games at 2100 within one day,
and the solved chess value fields compress to a few dozen fold coefficients
(top-32 = 81–87% energy). Purchased knowledge replaced by derived law, in
the most adversarial referee available.

## THE RULES (identical to every fold campaign)

1. ZERO PARAMETERS, ZERO AXIOMS beyond the One. A fix/component ships as
   counted, forced, or lossless — or it does not ship. No fitted numbers.
   No tuning against results (structurally impossible anyway: there is
   nothing to tune).
2. PRE-REGISTER every measurement: objects, transform, statistic, nulls,
   verdict rule — in writing, before the data. Negative results recorded
   in full.

   self-tests, e.g. F2-linear bit-reversal invariance).
4. The board/benchmark is the only referee. No deflationary imports, no
   inflationary claims: measured numbers, stated flat.
