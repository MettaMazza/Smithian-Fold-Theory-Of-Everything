# Fold Computational Laboratory constitution

## Authority

The Smithian Fold Theory main corpus is the laboratory's immutable authority. The frozen authority manifest records the exact source hashes used by a run. A mismatch stops receipt generation. A laboratory result cannot silently modify the corpus, the manifest, or the proof kernel.

The project does not use the old Desktop `TuringBot`, Unison AI, Fold Chess, Fold Go, or Fold Protein. It does not invoke a pretrained model, network service, stochastic sampler, or external autonomous process.

## Native machine

- The blank is the empty `One` form. It is not a numeric tape symbol.
- The tape alphabet contains only the two forced fibre labels `1` and `2`.
- A tape is an exact Fold word separated into a held observation record and a residual word.
- Reading is Fold observation.
- Writing is exact held-label substitution.
- Advance closes one residual distinction while retaining its predecessor label.
- Reverse is admitted only while that exact held label exists.
- Every transition is issued and rechecked by the immutable proof kernel.
- Host-language indices and empty collection counts are bookkeeping, not members of the Fold-value domain.

## Restrained autonomy

The controller may inspect only its declared tape and proof state. Its candidate actions come from the immutable kernel grammar. Every decision records its premises, complete alternatives, chosen action, transition certificate, resource bound, and hashes. An out-of-grammar choice stops with `FAILED_VERIFICATION`; exhaustion stops at the resource bound. Self-reproduction is internal tape construction only.

## Reversible and quantum mode

The quantum mode uses exact generated branch support and two exact phase labels. It contains no floating point, complex amplitude, irrational coordinate, probabilistic sampling, or imported collapse postulate.

- Complete depth-k word support is the superposition-equivalent state.
- Label opposition is the period-two phase action.
- Opposite-phase predecessors closing at a common image implement interference.
- Cartesian word composition supplies joint states.
- Controlled exact label action supplies entangling circuit behavior.
- Observation produces a selected exact word plus the complete retained measurement record.
- Width `2t+1` repetition words are exhaustively checked against every mask of weight at most `t` for the declared `t`.

This is an SFT computational semantics and proof laboratory. Physical claims require a separately authorized empirical comparison.

## Claim classes

`CLOSED_THEOREM_DEMONSTRATION` means the program instantiates an already registered main-corpus law and checks its exact consequence. It does not claim that finite execution alone proved the corpus law.

`EXACT_FINITE_INVESTIGATION` means every candidate inside an explicit generated boundary was executed. No statement outside that boundary is implied.

`FRONTIER_CONJECTURE` remains frontier work. Admission for corpus review requires all six items:

1. the problem definition is derived in Fold terms;
2. the candidate space is generated;
3. alternatives are eliminated;
4. either a depth-independent certificate exists or the finite boundary is explicit;
5. unfavorable controls pass;
6. an independent certificate reproduces the result.

Passing this gate permits review; it does not mutate the corpus or automatically close a theorem.

The four questions formerly listed by the laboratory as frontier-only were
subsequently admitted and closed by the independent main-corpus Steps 404–407.
The laboratory records them as `CLOSED_BY_MAIN_CORPUS`; it does not reclassify a
finite experiment as their proof. Conventional Turing-machine Busy Beaver,
external-language P versus NP, arbitrary external gate-basis lower bounds, and
stochastic physical fault thresholds remain outside those native theorems until
separate correspondence laws are derived.

## Evidence

Every receipt contains the exact initial description, dependency identifiers back to `One`, the complete trace or exhaustive census rows, time/space/branch/retained-information accounting, the accepted result, a deliberately false or tampered control, source hashes, trace hashes, the exhaustive boundary, and the claim class. The separate C verifier independently reproduces core finite consequences.
