## Status key

- **CLOSED** — derivation/form obligations closed inside the stated grammar.
- **EXECUTED** — current implementation receipt passed.
- **CONDITIONAL** — exact result inside a named finite grammar/bound.
- **CONSTRUCTION** — independently checkable witness located with disclosed target assistance.
- **FORWARD** — active forward-forcing derivation.
- **RANK-GATE** — competitive engineering gate, not proof validity.
- **DEVELOPMENT** — attributed implementation evidence; not Maria's finding,
  loss, endpoint, or a theoretical limit.
- **BRIDGE-OPEN** — internal result exists; named equivalence needs another theorem.
- **VOID** — rejected by the programme's own constitution.
